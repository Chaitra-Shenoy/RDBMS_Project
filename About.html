Idea Behind "RESTAURANT FINDER"

The idea is based of the very common problem that people face when they shift or visit a new place for the first time. This website makes it easier for people to browse for their choice of restaurants or cafes without having to check for ratings or reviews in multiple websites.
Our website also allows people to reserve tables which further eases the troubles that people often face when it come to dining in restaurants specially in the weekends.

Crediblity of the website

The website is constantly updated and changes are made as and when they occur. Any changes with respect to the prices , reviews and ratings are closely monitored and updated to provide the user with the best possible experience.
In case of any inconvinence or problems faced such a booking not confirmed or any problems with respect to accessing the restaurant information there is our help desk available to solve your problems as soon as possible.
The aim of this website is to make the lives of our users as easy as possible.

What makes this website different from the other websites available on the internet?

This website may have been launched less than a year ago, but has managed to create a mark for itself. The easy to use manner in which it is designed pleases one and all. Also the accuracy of the information provided to the customers add on to the reasons why this website is so widely used. This website currently holds information about the restaurants around Mangalore,Udupi,Manipal.
But we plan on expanding this website to provide results for restauarnts all over Karnataka. It's a work in progress as of now. Keep checking this page for further updates.
